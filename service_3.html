ROAD SHOW

Road shows form an important part of many corporate external marketing programs. The ability to take a message around the country in a matter of days is very powerful and logistically very challenging for all concerned. We have been involved in operating multi-city road shows across India.Road shows offer a huge amount of visibility, be it the most conventional or the most contemporary exclusively depending upon the respective product.Craftworld corporate event company in Mumbai have been actively conducting roadshows across the country.

MALL PROMOTION

Mall promotions involve exclusive displays for your brands/services which can reach your preferred target group.Malls have become the hub of consumer activity, and hence promotions in these malls are a crucial part of the marketing mix for any company. It is an effective means of selling brand identity. It uses various forms of display to entice consumers who are present in the mall or shopping area.Apart from displays, interactive activities and games, designed within the framework of the brand identity, can be used to lure consumers and generate awareness about the brand.Access the photo gallery to view some of the mall promotions that we’ve done before.

IN SHOP PROMOTION

In-shop promotions, though similar to mall promotions, occur inside shopping complexes and retail stores. Here, each brand is promoted, either with leaflets or fliers (using promoters to talk to consumers), or else, certain parts of the store are used to depict the brand’s selling points, and thus, consumers are lured.

CORPORATE PROMOTION

Office promotions are done, with a business-to-business orientation. Kiosks are set up at office premises and fliers and other direct mailers are distributed to create awareness about new services and product launches that the company wishes to portray to its employees or customers.

SOCIETY PROMOTION

If the consumers don’t come to your product, take the product to the consumers instead.Society promotions are an innovative method of using product display, and other forms of promotion, in certain housing societies, of a particular level, which come under the desired target group for your company’s brand.We do proper display unit, with product ranges, varieties, can be used, and they can be placed inside the premises of the society, where consumers, can touch, feel, and make use of the product as and when the situation demands.
